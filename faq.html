Q & A

Have a question? It may be already answered below.

We're based out of Jericho, Vermont. Travel is our love language! Our work has brought us all across the U.S. as well as India, Mexico, France, Greece, Sweden, Norway, Italy, Iceland, The Dominican Republic, Thailand, Vietnam, The Bahamas, Ireland, Canada, and we can’t wait to add more to that list.

We typically deliver sessions in about 1 week, weddings in about 2 weeks. We deliver sneak peeks of every session & wedding, so you will receive a handful of images before the full gallery delivery - we know first hand how exciting it is to see the photos, and how long 2 weeks can feel.

We deliver all images taken with the exception of duplicate + test photos. There is no cap on the images delivered. These are your once in a lifetime memories, and if there is a great photo taken, we believe that you deserve to have it. We enhance all images to match our editing style that you see throughout our website. All images are delivered in JPEG format for easy printing!

This is the number one question / fear that we hear from everyone! Don't worry about posing or pretending. For real though, everyone is photogenic as long as they're out of their own head. We're after the true expressions! We won't tell you exactly what to do and how to smile. The best thing you can do is to come to the shoot with an open mind and open heart. We will do everything in our power to get you feeling comfortable and ready to rock it! We'll play games, listen to music, and make dad jokes. It'll be a blast!

If you're located in Vermont, you’re in luck! Travel fees are included in most SoCal wedding and adventure sessions. If you’re outside of the Northeastern States area, we will calculate flight, lodging, gas or rental car fees and add that into the package price. We don’t believe you should be charged a big flat rate travel fee. You should only be paying for our exact travel costs and the photo package that fits your needs. Nothing more and no hidden fees added on at the end.

We use Canon 5D Mark IV digital SLR bodies, Canon professional speedlights and Canon L-series lenses. We also photograph with film and we use Contax645 medium format camera. We process all of our photographs on current Macintosh computers using Adobe Lightroom, Photoshop and other professional photography software. We use a color-managed workflow for highly accurate color. We invest regularly to stay current on camera equipment, computer equipment/software and education to insure you receive very high quality photographs.
